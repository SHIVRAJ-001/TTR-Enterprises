About Us

Trusted Coding & Marking Solutions Since 2017

TTR Enterprises is a leading wholesale trader and service provider for industrial inkjet coding machines and consumables, headquartered at Ambedkar Chowk, Jwalapur, Haridwar.

Founded by proprietor Trilok Singh, we have grown to become the go-to destination for manufacturers, FMCG companies, and packaging units across Haridwar and Uttarakhand. We supply, install, and service CIJ printers, TIJ printers, laser coders, and all associated consumables including inks, solvents, and spare parts.
